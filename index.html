My Philosophy

Automation First

I believe in eliminating toil. From infrastructure provisioning with Terraform to application deployment with CI/CD, every manual process is an opportunity for robust automation. This ensures consistency, reduces errors, and frees up time for high-impact engineering.

Pragmatic Security

Security isn't an afterthought; it's a foundational requirement. I build systems with a "secure by design" approach, implementing principles like least-privilege IAM, mTLS encryption, and VPC firewalls to create resilient, production-grade environments.

Cost-Conscious Architecture

Exceptional engineering is also economically efficient. I specialize in designing "zero-cost" or cost-optimized solutions by leveraging free tiers, serverless technologies, and resource automation, ensuring that scalability doesn't lead to unpredictable expenses.
